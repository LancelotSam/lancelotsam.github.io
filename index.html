About

The primary challenge addressed by this project is the inconvenience caused by advertisements on YouTube, as well as the potential loss of favorite videos due to removal from the platform. By offering a free and accessible solution for downloading YouTube content, this project aims to enhance user experience and mitigate these common frustrations. However, it's important to note that the project does not resolve any potential intellectual property issues related to video sharing and is part of my school project.
